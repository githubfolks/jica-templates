Procurement Committee (PC)

The Procurement Committee plays a critical role in ensuring transparent, fair, and efficient procurement of goods, works, and services for Tripura JICA SCATFORM Project.

Roles and Responsibilities of the Procurement Committee

  • Ensure procurement activities follow JICA's and state government's procurement guidelines, including transparency, competitiveness, and value for money.
  • Approve draft tender documents (RFPs, RFQs, bid documents) before publication and ensure bid documents clearly define specifications, eligibility, evaluation criteria, and contract conditions.
  • Supervise the opening of bids (technical and financial) to ensure fairness and proper documentation and constitute or approve Bid Evaluation Committees (BEC) to carry out technical and financial evaluations.
  • Ensure no conflict of interest, collusion, or manipulation during the award process and approve the final contract document and authorize the signing of the contract.
