Grievances Redressal Committee

The Grievance Redressal Committee (GRC) plays a vital role in addressing complaints, resolving disputes, and maintaining transparency and accountability in the Tripura JICA SCATFORM Project. It acts as a formal mechanism to ensure affected individuals or communities have access to a fair and timely resolution process.

Roles and Responsibilities of the Grievances Redressal Committee

  • Ensure all grievances are properly recorded, acknowledged, and categorized (e.g., related to procurement, community participation, forest rights, livelihood activities, payments, or project impacts).
  • Review and investigate each grievance or complaint objectively, using facts, documents, and consultations with stakeholders.
  • Recommend corrective actions, compensation, or policy adjustments wherever necessary and ensure timely resolution.
  • Maintain a Grievance Register/Logbook to track all complaints, actions taken, and resolutions. And prepare periodic reports of grievances to the Project Steering Committee or Executive Committee.
