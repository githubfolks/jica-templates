NTFP & Non- NTFP Based Product

NTFP and non-NTFP livelihood activities under the Tripura SCATFORM Project serve as powerful tools for poverty reduction, forest conservation, and rural empowerment. By combining sustainable resource use with livelihood security, the project ensures a balanced approach to forest ecosystem management and community development in the fragile landscapes of Tripura.

Examples of NTFP Activities

  • Bamboo and cane (handicrafts, mats, furniture)
  • Medicinal and aromatic plants (Tulsi, Amla, Haritaki, etc.)
  • Wild edible fruits and seeds
  • Lichens and mushrooms
  • Honey and wax
  • Leaves (e.g., Sal, Banana for plate making)

Examples of Non-NTFP Activities

  • Backyard poultry and goat rearing
  • Fish farming
  • Mushroom cultivation
  • Nursery raising (medicinal and horticultural plants)
  • Horticulture & agroforestry (banana, pineapple, lemon, etc.)
  • Leaf plate making, tailoring, food processing
  • Vermicomposting & organic farming
