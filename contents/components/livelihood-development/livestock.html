Livestock

Livestock-based livelihood interventions under the Tripura SCATFORM Project are a sustainable, inclusive, and practical approach to rural development. By aligning animal husbandry with natural resource management, the project ensures that economic development does not come at the cost of ecological degradation. Instead, it promotes a holistic model of forest-dependent community upliftment and environmental sustainability.

Objectives of Livestock Activities in SCATFORM

  • Reduce over-dependence on forest-based income (e.g., firewood, timber, NTFP collection)
  • Improve food and nutritional security through milk, meat, and poultry products
  • Empower Self-Help Groups (SHGs) and JFMC members, especially women
