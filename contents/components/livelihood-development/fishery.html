Fishery

Fishery activity in the Tripura SCATFORM Project is a model of integrated livelihood and resource management. It not only improves household income but also adds value to the water conservation infrastructure created under the project. Through technical training, input support, and participatory implementation, fisheries under SCATFORM promote a resilient, inclusive, and sustainable development pathway for forest-fringe communities in Tripura.

Objective of Fishery Activities in SCATFORM

  • Enhance livelihood opportunities for local communities, especially SHGs and JFMC members
  • Promote sustainable use of water resources created through SMC interventions
  • Support nutritional security and income generation
  • Encourage community ownership and participation in resource management
