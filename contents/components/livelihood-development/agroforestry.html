Agroforestry

Agroforestry under the Tripura SCATFORM Project is a people-centric and eco-friendly approach to managing degraded lands and catchments. By blending livelihood development with environmental restoration, agroforestry ensures that the economic needs of communities are met without compromising the health of forests and ecosystems. It is a sustainable bridge between agriculture and forestry, deeply aligned with the goals of SCATFORM.

Objectives of Agroforestry under SCATFORM

  • Promote sustainable land use in catchment and degraded areas
  • Support livelihood diversification for forest-dependent communities
  • Improve soil fertility, moisture retention, and biodiversity
  • Reduce dependency on forests for fuelwood, fodder, and timber
  • Integrate with climate resilience and carbon sequestration efforts
