SMC Structures

Soil Moisture Conservation (SMC) structures are integral to the Tripura SCATFORM Project, supporting its goal of catchment area restoration, forest health improvement, and climate resilience. These structures play a crucial role in managing natural resources sustainably, especially in hilly, erosion-prone, and degraded landscapes.

Details of Nursery Created under Tripura SCATFORM Project.

Type of Structure Purpose
Contour Trenches In-situ moisture retention on slopes
Check Dams/Nala Plugs Arresting water flow in small streams
Gully Control Structures Stabilize eroded stream beds
Percolation Tanks Groundwater recharge
Earthen/Stone Bunds Slow down water flow on slopes
Water Absorption Trenches Improve infiltration near plantations
Recharge Pits Collect rainwater for percolation
