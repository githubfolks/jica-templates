Forestry Integrated Geospatial Solution

Tripura JICA SCATFORM Project aims to improve forest quality in the targeted catchment areas where the focus on catchment protection is through sustainable forest management, soil moisture conservation and livelihood development. For effective planning, monitoring, management and reporting of the project activities as well alignment these activities across the department.

PMU-SCATFORM has signed a 5-year contract with M/S NeoGeoInfo Technologies Pvt Ltd to Design, Develop and Maintain Forestry Integrated Geospatial Solution (FIGS) which is a spatially smart system to ensure holistic decision making.

The FIGS system provides an end-to-end geospatial solution for the decision making to ensure improved forest quality with focus on catchment protection through afforestation, soil and moisture conservation and nature-based livelihood development. The FIGS system solutions and applications focus includes regular management and monitoring of afforestation, plantation, soil moisture conservation works, nursery management, biodiversity changes, biophysical changes, livelihood, agroforestry as well as alignment with the routine activities of the forest department for working plan management, forest fire monitoring, shifting cultivation monitoring, encroachment and illegal felling monitoring.

The Overall task includes preparation of BFBP, creation of spatial data inputs, using remote sensing and mapping, developing requisite solutions and applications for different forestry works for the project and the department integrated with MIS and existing GIS and Non- GIS Systems of Forest Department.
