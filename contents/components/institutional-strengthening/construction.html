Construction

Asset Construction is a core component of the Tripura SCATFORM (Sustainable Catchment Forest Management) Project, which is supported by JICA and implemented by the Government of Tripura. These activities aim to strengthen the institutional capacity to ensure effective and sustainable forest and catchment management. Constructions of Offices like DFO Office, Range Office, Guest House, Multi Utility Centre etc are core component of the Project.
