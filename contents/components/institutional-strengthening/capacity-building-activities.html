Capacity Building Activities

Capacity building is a core component of the Tripura SCATFORM (Sustainable Catchment Forest Management) Project, which is supported by JICA and implemented by the Government of Tripura. These activities aim to strengthen the institutional, technical, and community-level capacities to ensure effective and sustainable forest and catchment management.

Objectives of Capacity Building

  • To enhance the skills and knowledge of stakeholders involved in forest and natural resource management.
  • To promote community participation in JFMC activities and decision-making.
  • To build technical expertise in soil and water conservation, agroforestry, NTFP, and livelihood activities.
  • To strengthen the institutional capacity of Forest Department officials, JFMCs and SHGs.

Key Target Groups

  • Forest Department Officials (Field Staff to DFOs)
  • Joint Forest Management Committees (JFMCs)
  • Self-Help Groups (SHGs)
  • Village Development Committees (VDCs)
