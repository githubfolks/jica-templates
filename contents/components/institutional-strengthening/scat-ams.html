SCAT-AMS (SCATFORM Attendance Monitoring System)

The SCAT-AMS is a robust in-house application designed to effectively monitor project staff and their activities in real time. By capturing the actual Geo-fenced location and timestamp of staff members, the system ensures complete accuracy in attendance calculation for payroll processing. This has eliminated the need for manual record-keeping and significantly streamlined the attendance management process.

Hosted securely on the JICA server, the solution is available on both mobile and web platforms, ensuring data security, integrity, and real-time accessibility. Unlike conventional systems, SCAT-AMS delivers dashboard-based insights instantly, making it a uniquely effective tool for field-based projects.

Beyond attendance tracking, the system is equipped with several advanced features:

  • Task Scheduling - Enables project authorities to assign tasks directly to field staff.
  • Leave Management - Integrated module to streamline leave requests and approvals.
  • Daily Notes - Allows staff to record personal notes for better self-management.
  • Query-Based Reports - Attendance and availability records can be retrieved for any given date.
  • Self-Monitoring - Staff members can view and track their own records, enhancing transparency.

Together, these features provide a holistic view of project staff availability and activity on a daily basis, while improving efficiency, accountability, and planning across the project.
