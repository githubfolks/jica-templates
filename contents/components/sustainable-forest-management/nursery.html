Nursery

Nurseries play a foundational role in sustainable forest management (SFM) by providing healthy, high-quality seedlings needed for forest restoration, reforestation, and plantation development. Without nurseries, large-scale and long-term forest regeneration efforts would not be possible.

The role of nurseries in the SCATFORM Project is not just technical but transformational. They form the backbone of plantation and ecological restoration activities, while also acting as tools for community empowerment and sustainable development. Through well-managed nurseries, the project ensures long-term forest sustainability, community participation, and climate resilience across Tripura's forested landscapes.

The project has the objective to make available of seedlings for plantation, ecological restoration and community involvement to address above these the nursery activities have been divided in 3 categories:

  • High- tech nursery (HTN)
  • Central Nursery (CN)
  • Decentralized peoples Nursery (DCPN)

Objective of Hi-Tech Nursery

  • Production of the highest quality seedlings and other plant propagule, especially seedlings of species requiring intensive care such as some bamboo and cane species with the help of advanced technology.
  • Research on propagation methods of difficult but economically important plants.
  • Standardization of propagation methodology of rare/endangered/threatened plant species.

Milestones

In the project 3 nos. Hi-tech Nurseries have been developed in 3 different districts namely North Tripura, West Tripura & Gomati. These nurseries are focused on the identification, propagation, and conservation of endangered and threatened plant species.

Objective of Central Nursery

The specific objective to develop the CN is to improving existing nurseries (central nurseries of the state) is to produce a large number of seedlings with reliable quality for establishment of plantation under the project.

Milestones

Seven nos of CN have been established during this project period throughout the different district. Names are South, Sepahijala, khowai, Dhalai, Unakoti & North.
