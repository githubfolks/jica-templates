JFMC / EDC

A Joint Forest Management Committee (JFMC) is a community-based institution established under the Joint Forest Management (JFM) approach in India, where local communities and the Forest Department work together to protect, manage, and sustainably use forest resources.

In the Tripura SCATFORM Project, JFMCs are the central implementation bodies at the village level, playing a key role in planning, executing, and monitoring project interventions such as plantations, soil and water conservation, livelihoods (e.g., agroforestry, NTFP, livestock), and ecotourism.
